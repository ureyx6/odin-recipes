Chicken Quesadilla

Description

This is a quick and easy Chicken Quesadilla Recipe that will leave you craving for more. With its ease and great flavor you will enjoy every bit of this chicken quesadilla.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Shredded Chicken
  • Diced Pepper
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Shredded Cheese

Steps

  1. Microwave Tortillas
  2. Mix chicken, peppers, and taco seasoning cook for 2 minutes
  3. Spread evenly on tortilla
  4. Cook for 30 seconds to 1 minute, slice then serve
