Lasagna

Description

This is my favorite Lasagna ever. This Lasagna is a very tasteful yet non overwhelming mix of flavors. It packs a punch while still managing to taste good.

Ingredients

  • Sweet Italian Sauage
  • Lean Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Sugar
  • Spices
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Make meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make ricotta mixture
  4. Layer Lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let rest
